# Support Process

## Contacting support
Users can contact support via:
- Support portal
- Email
- Live chat (Pro and Enterprise plans)

## Response times
Typical response times:
- Free: up to 72 hours
- Pro: up to 24 hours
- Enterprise: up to 4 hours

## Account recovery
If a user cannot access their account and password reset fails,
support may perform manual account recovery after identity verification.
