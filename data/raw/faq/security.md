# Security FAQ

## Two-factor authentication (2FA)
We strongly recommend enabling two-factor authentication.
Supported methods:
- Authenticator apps (TOTP)
- SMS codes

To enable 2FA, go to **Settings → Security → Two-factor authentication**.

## Failed login attempts
After **5 failed login attempts**, your account will be temporarily locked.
The lock is automatically removed after **10 minutes**.

## Password requirements
Passwords must:
- Be at least **10 characters long**
- Contain letters and numbers
- Not be reused from previous passwords
