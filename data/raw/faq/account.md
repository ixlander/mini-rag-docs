# Account FAQ

## Reset password
To reset your password, open **Settings → Security → Reset password**.
You will receive a confirmation email with a reset link.
The reset link expires after **15 minutes**.

If you do not receive the email, check your spam folder or contact support.

## Change email address
To change your email address:
1. Go to **Settings → Account**.
2. Enter the new email address.
3. Confirm the change via the verification email.

Email changes take effect immediately after confirmation.

## Delete account
Account deletion is permanent.
To delete your account, submit a request via **Settings → Account → Delete account**.
Your data will be removed within **30 days**.
